Twitch beats Lord Helix

January 1, 2014


After 13 days, Twitch Plays Pokemon has successfully beaten Red and Pokemon Crystal. After 13 days traveling through the region of Unova and Kanto. In a tense 20 minute battle, our hero AJ was down to his last pokemon Lazorgatr (Feraligatr). Taking down each of Red's pokemons, it was fitting that he ended up facing off against Lord Helix (Omastar) and finally Bird Jesus (Pidgeot). AJ would emerge triumphant, signalling the end of the Pokemon Crystal journey. Twitch Plays Pokemon will be continuing in 6 days where they will begin their journey into Pokemon Emerald.
